About Me

I am a first-year PhD student at Cornell interested in solving theoretical problems of industrial relevence -- specifically in the field of Programming Languages. My research interests include domain-specific language design, type systems, formal semantics, algebra, graph theory, and formal verification. I also enjoy linguistics and ballroom dance.

I am currently working to publish my undergraduate thesis work (with Michael Greenberg at Pomona College) on Finite-Trace Linear Temporal Logic and Temporal Netkat.
